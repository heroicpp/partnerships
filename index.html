Brand

Brand Guidelines & Rules

Do

  • Use approved Heroic logos without modification
  • Link directly to heroic.us or philosophersnotes.com
  • Share genuine personal experiences
  • Disclose your partner relationship (FTC)
  • Use your unique tracking link for all promotions
  • Match the brand tone: aspirational, practical, credible

Don't

  • Modify, recolor, or distort Heroic logos
  • Make false or exaggerated product claims
  • Bid on "Heroic" or "PhilosophersNotes" in paid ads
  • Send spam or unsolicited bulk emails
  • Use Heroic branding in misleading contexts
  • Offer unauthorized discounts or cash incentives
